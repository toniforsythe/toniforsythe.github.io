Introduction

Forsythe Events

Forsythe Events is a full-service event planning buiness located in San Luis Obispo, CA. We specialize in event planning, design, and coordination for the most imporant celebrations in your life. Our office is based in San Luis Obispo but we offer our services all over the central coast.

Target audience

Viewers of Forsythe Events are looking for someone to help them execute a beautiful celebration in their life. This includes newly engaged couples planning weddings, families planning birthday parties, the list goes on and on.

People who come to Forsythe Events want to celebate in style. They need help choosing which direction to go when it comes to planning a big event and executing their vision.

Weddings

Full Planning

So you want a beautiful wedding but don't know or want to figure out every single detail? Our full planning package is what you're looking for. With this plan we'll take care of everything from the initial vision to the design and planning to day of coordination.

Design & Coordination

Our design and coordination plan is aimed to help you figure out the major compoents of your wedding. We'll take the time to strategize and organize all your wants and needs, as well as design your wedding exactly how you want it.

Basic Event Management

This is our simplist plan. It's designed for those who want to tak care of the little things and be more involved in the wedding planning, but need help with overall management and organization. Our team will be there to help book the imporant vendors, make sure everything on the to-do list gets checked off, and the wedding itself goes to plan.
