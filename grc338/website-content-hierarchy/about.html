About Us

Enjoy a glass of wine or your favorite cocktail with dinner in our gracious dining room or while relaxing with family and friends in our urban bar. We would be delighted to be the place where you choose to unwind from a busy day or a stressful week or to celebrate a birthday, anniversary, a good day or the end of a bad day. We feature wine country cuisine to complement the Central Coasts' wine region. Each menu item has been created to pair nicely with a fine glass of wine. Our chefs select the freshest and finest ingredients available, many from local growers and producers. We have a wonderful variety of savory appetizers/small plates and entrees to excite your palate and a selection of delectable desserts to satisfy your sweet tooth. Extensive vegetarian options as well as children options are available. Our wine list features an extensive array of still and sparkling wines from some of the finest local, national, and international wine regions including California, France, Spain and Italy. A wide variety of craft and specialty beers are also available. Coast is located in the historic J.P. Andrews Building at the corner of Monterey and Osos Streets across from the Court St. Center in Downtown San Luis Obispo on California's Central Coast on Pacific Highway 1.
