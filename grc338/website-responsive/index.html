How It Started

We feature wine country cuisine to complement the Central Coasts' wine region. Each menu item has been created to pair nicely with a fine glass of wine. Our chefs select the freshest and finest ingredients available, many from local growers and producers.

We have a wonderful variety of savory appetizers/small plates and entrees to excite your palate and a selection of delectable desserts to satisfy your sweet tooth. Extensive vegetarian options as well as children options are available.
